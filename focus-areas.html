Research Domains

Integrated focus areas.

Each focus area is designed to be methodologically rigorous, clinically relevant, and scalable across large health-system datasets.

◎

Radiomics and Imaging AI

We develop explainable imaging pipelines that extract quantitative phenotypes from CT, CMR, echocardiography, retinal imaging, and other clinical imaging modalities.

  • Opportunistic cardiovascular and cardiometabolic phenotyping
  • Calcium scoring, fat quantification, vascular phenotypes, and organ-level imaging biomarkers
  • Deep learning segmentation and radiomics for risk prediction
CT AI CMR Echo Retinal imaging
▣

EHR Analytics and Deep Phenotyping

We transform structured and unstructured EHR data into computable phenotypes that support discovery, risk modeling, and clinical decision support.

  • Longitudinal disease trajectory modeling
  • LLM-enabled clinical text phenotyping
  • Integration of diagnoses, labs, medications, procedures, notes, and outcomes
EHR NLP LLMs Clinical trajectories
◍

Exposome Science

We link clinical data with high-resolution measures of place, environment, climate, and social context to understand how external exposures shape cardiovascular and metabolic health.

  • Air pollution, heat, greenspace, walkability, light pollution, and deprivation
  • Neighborhood-level risk signatures and environmental fingerprints
  • Place-based disparities and resilience research
Air pollution Heat Greenspace Neighborhoods
⌁

Biosignals and ECG AI

We build artificial intelligence models that use ECGs and physiologic signals to detect subclinical disease, predict outcomes, and improve screening.

  • ECG image and waveform-based AI
  • Prediction of heart failure, coronary disease, valvular disease, and cardiometabolic risk
  • Integration with telemetry and continuous physiologic signals
ECG AI Telemetry Biosignals Screening
⇄

Precision Prevention

We develop models and decision-support strategies that help identify the right patient, at the right time, for the right preventive intervention.

  • Personalized cardiovascular risk prediction
  • Imaging-guided prevention strategies
  • Clinical decision support for risk factor treatment and preventive care
Risk prediction Prevention Decision support
↳

Outcomes and Implementation Science

We evaluate the clinical, economic, and equity impact of computational tools using real-world evidence and learning health system methods.

  • Comparative effectiveness and real-world evidence
  • Cost-effectiveness and population impact modeling
  • Fairness, generalizability, and implementation evaluation

Disease Areas

Disease focus areas.

C3PH advances precision health through artificial intelligence, multimodal imaging, exposomics, and large-scale clinical data to better understand, predict, and prevent cardiometabolic and aging-related diseases.

Atherosclerotic Cardiovascular Disease

Risk prediction, imaging-derived plaque and calcification phenotypes, prevention strategies, and outcomes modeling.

Diabetes

Prediction of incident diabetes, cardiometabolic trajectories, treatment response, and environmental risk modifiers.

Dementia

Vascular, environmental, metabolic, and imaging signatures linked to cognitive decline and aging-related brain health.

Metabolic-Associated Liver Disease

AI-enabled liver fat phenotyping, cardiometabolic risk stratification, and longitudinal outcomes research.

Adiposity

Body composition, ectopic fat, visceral and epicardial adiposity, obesity phenotypes, and precision treatment pathways.

Chronic Kidney Disease

Cardio-kidney-metabolic risk prediction, kidney disease trajectories, and integrated prevention strategies.
